Our Concept

Being in the heart of Sheung Wan inspired us to embody the soul of Hong Kong into our space and products. Inspired by dim sum and traditional Chinese dessert soup, prepared using french pastry techniques our cakes all embody elements of Hong Kong in them, we hope to draw on your memories and remind you to celebrate the unique and beautiful city we live in.

What sets Poem Patisserie apart from other cake shops are our originality, and our exquisite eye for details, this is what distinguishes and defines us. From special celebrations, to corporate events, or even for your own enjoyment, we create bite sized and sharing sized cakes for all occasions. We will fulfill all your dietary needs to ensure you have the opportunity to experience all the special flavours we've put together to inspire your taste buds. Poem's everlasting goal is to create the the most extraordinary flavours in the most beautiful designs.
